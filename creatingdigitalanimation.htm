  Creating Digital Animation  
     
 

In digital animation we looked at two different animations and studied whether they were fit for purpose. We looked at what the animations set out to achieve and decided if they achieved their goal. We looked at the strengths and weaknesses and if they could be improved in some way. We then used this to design an animation that was related to health and safety. My choice was an animation to stop smoking as I had decided it was time for me to stop smoking. Being a smoker at the time I knew the feeling of having to rush from the classroom, have a smoke and get back to the class on time and sometimes having to stand outside in the wind and the rain. The feeling was very stressful and I imagined what non smokers probably  thought of smokers going through all this just to have a smoke. My idea was to put together an animation with anti-smoking messages going on in the background that the viewer would acknowledge subconsciously, while the main character would be dancing and smoking to an eminem track called Slim shady. I kept the first part of the song with the introduction in as it was but then I sampled part of the song and looped so I had an empty tune to add some Rap lyrics about not smoking. I did get quite far with the animation but it was not quite finished. I had all the graphics prepared that I did in Adobe Photoshop. I had the music done and the Rap lyrics written but then run into problems with Adobe Animate. The main problem was as I imported parts into Adobe Animate it knocked everything out of sync and it was a case of me Reimporting everything into Adobe Animate and starting again. But I ran out of time so I presented what I had finished and luckily it was enough to get a grade. So all I can present you with here is the idea and a screenshot of the animation. To be honest I needed much more time than what was available to me to master Adobe Animate.
