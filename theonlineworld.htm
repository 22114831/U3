The Online World
     
In the unit Online World we researched everything about the internet and how data was sent and received.
Also we looked at the hardware that connected everything together on networks including the internet,
We looked at different types of connections to the internet by different companies. We also looked at protocols that are just sets of rules about how things are done over the internet. We had an exam at the end of the
research in which I got a pass with 29 points, which was one point off merit. I was disappointed with this until I realised that a large number of students failed it the first time around. Then I realised it must have been more difficult than I realised.
