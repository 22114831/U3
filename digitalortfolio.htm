Digital Portfolio
 
The digital portfolio is this website and its collection of information about the subjects that have been completed as part of the course including maths and english that were additional to the course. While on the course each subject is referred to as a unit number. However I have labelled them with their proper worded title. The idea is to let you see some examples of the work I have completed while on the course. Some are just text descriptions written by myself. While there are a lot of graphic images I've created as part of the course. There is also a video and two audio files.
I did intend to show actual assignments that I have completed as part of the course. However I thought it might be long winded and a little bit boring to look through the complete assinments. So I decided to go with a description of the unit and a description of the tasks that I completed as part of the unit. Also where possible I have added some graphics that are related to the unit in question.
