GCSE Maths Intermediate
     
I did GCSE Maths to improve my grade. When I originally did GCSE Maths I was put in for a level where the highest grade I could achieve was Grade C. I had the opportunity to improve my grade. I would have liked to be put in for Grade a but the highest the college will put you in for is Grade B. I think that is because of the amount of work that is needed in such a short time. Usually it would be a few years of study but we have to learn it in about 9 months.
So the course is quite intense. You really have to revise hard. I've not had my results but I'm fairly confident I will achieve a grade B.
