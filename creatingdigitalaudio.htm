  Creating Digital Audio

The creating digital audio unit was about creating pieces of audio. One would be an audio advert for a comedy show and the other would be a podcast whose theme would be scientific. We started by looking at two pieces of audio and looking at their effect on the listener. I chose one piece of music and an audio clip from a film. We looked at their strengths and weaknesses and their effect on the listener and whether they were fit for purpose. Also we looked at ways that they could be improved.

My comedy advert was from a past comedy show that was one of my favourite shows. However I had to be careful because the content today may be deemed as inappropriate. But I liked the challenge of doing the comedy advert without offending anybody and I think I achieved that. For the scientific podcast I found an article that was interesting from an online news paper. From this I came up with a show name of Strange But True. I went about writing a summary of the news article and from that wrote a script. I recorded the audio in small chunks using Audacity software. I created a music / effects audio track that came from one of my sample cds. I looped the sound and allowed it to play the entire length of the Strange But True audio track. I manipulated this using Adobe audition. At the beginning the sound played quite loud then I faded it so it was still there but in the background and quiet enough that it didn't distract the listener from listening to the spoken words. At the end I faded it back in so it played quite loud till the end of the track. The spoken word and music / effects sound were mixed within Adobe Audition.
